1 — Dark Study

Late-night research energy
Deep blue-charcoal base with warm gold accents. Evokes: a lit desk in a dark library, brass reading lamps, thesis drafts at 2am. The gold isn't flashy — it's the color of worn book spines. Says: "I think deeply about things."

2 — Ink & Stone

Editorial warmth, European restraint
Warm parchment base with deep brown and charcoal. Evokes: letterpress prints, Viennese coffee houses, well-typeset journals. No bright accents — the accent IS the darkness of the ink against warm stone. Says: "I let the work speak."

3 — Midnight Botanical

Not Škoda green — forest-at-dusk green
Near-black base with a muted sage-green accent. Evokes: moss on old stone, a calm walk through data, the moment before an insight lands. The green is desaturated enough to feel intellectual, not environmental. Says: "I find patterns others miss."
